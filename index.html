You’re probably reading this article because you want to know more about solar energy. Solar energy has many advantages over other sources of power generation: – It is clean; no air pollution or water pollution like with fossil fuels – There are zero-emissions unlike in the case of burning coal – No fuel costs as long as sunlight is available – When there is no sunlight available (nighttime or during a storm), solar energy can be stored in batteries for use later on – It is a renewable resource; the sun will continue to shine whether we use solar energy or not
